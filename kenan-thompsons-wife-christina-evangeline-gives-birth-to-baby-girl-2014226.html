Kenan Thompson's Wife Christina Evangeline Gives Birth to Baby Girl

It's a girl for Kenan Thompson! The Saturday Night Live funnyman and his wife, Christina Evangeline, welcomed their first child, a baby girl, on Friday, June 20, a source confirms to Us Weekly.

According to TMZ, which first reported the news, little Georgia Marie Thompson weighed in at 6 pounds when she was born at 10:30 a.m. in New York City. The new parents are said to be exhausted, but ecstatic.

Thompson, 36, first revealed his wife's pregnancy during an appearance on Late Night With Seth Meyers in early March. "I'm about to be a daddy!" he said at the time. "That's right! Papa Kenan."
