Who is Andrew Ridgeley?

Andrew John Ridgeley, born on January 26, 1963, is an intriguing English singer, songwriter, musician, and record producer who rose to fame in the vibrant 1980s as one half of the beloved musical duo Wham!

Together with his partner, George Michael, Ridgeley became a prominent figure in the music scene, captivating audiences worldwide with their catchy pop tunes and dynamic performances.

During their time together, Wham! achieved remarkable success, topping the charts with hits like “Wake Me Up Before You Go-Go,” “Careless Whisper,” and “Last Christmas.”

Their infectious blend of pop, soul, and dance music established them as icons of the era.

Andrew Ridgeley, the former member of Wham!, is alive and currently resides in Wadebridge, Cornwall. Following Wham!’s disbandment, Ridgeley chose to embrace a more private lifestyle. Alongside his former partner, Keren Woodward from Bananarama, he sought solace in the tranquility of the countryside. While he firmly dismissed the possibility of a reunion, he remains committed to commemorating Wham!’s milestones in diverse ways.

Preserving the essence of Wham! holds profound significance for Ridgeley, as he believes it aligns with the desires of George Michael, affectionately known as Yog. As he approaches 60, Ridgeley envisions an active future, poised to embark on a fresh chapter in his career.
