An Electrifying Performance Of Secret Love Song By Jason Derulo And The Biggest Girl Group In The Wo

Little Mix and Jason Derulo stole the show at the Capital Jingle Bell Ball as they showered the audience with perfect high notes and an endless amount of beautiful harmonies.

As it was already expected, the girls announced that ‘Secret Love Song’ is in fact the next single off their new album Get Weird. A perfect choice (we believe) to come after the two amazing successes of ‘Black Magic’ and ‘Love Me Like You’.

Amazingly, both ‘Black Magic’ and ‘Love Me Like You’ are still in the UK charts after 21 and 10 weeks respectively. Their single ‘Wings’ still holds the title as their longest song on the charts with 25!

The girl group immediately cast a worldwide trend on Twitter (#LittleMixAtCapitalJBB) after their performance was over. Fans and non-fans raved about the duo’s singing abilities.

While they did announce it is the new UK & Ireland single, we hope it will be a worldwide single as well. This is the perfect song for a worldwide release not just because of the high reaching vocals but because it is a universally relatable song.

Let’s also not forget about ‘Secret Love Song Pt. II, the beautiful LGBT anthem that could also be released as a side single with ‘Secret Love Song’.
