Who won big at the Kids' Choice Awards? Here is your full list of this year's winners

Fully equipped with plenty of laughs, fun and green slime, the 2017 Nickelodeon Kids’ Choice Awards took place on Saturday evening at the USC Galen Center in Los Angeles.

The annual award show, hosted by actor and WWE star John Cena featured musical performances by Machine Gun Kelly, Camila Cabello and Little Mix, who picked up the award for Favorite Global Music Star.

Want to know who else pop culture’s youngest fans voted for? Check out the complete list of KCA winners below.
