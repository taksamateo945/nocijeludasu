Now 92: The Track List

Now 92, the latest installment in the Now That’s What I Call Music! compilation album series is released tomorrow (November 20th).

Packed with 46 smash hits, the album features eight number one singles, including tracks from Sam Smith, One Direction, Justin Bieber, Charlie Puth and Meghan Trainor, Jess Glynne, Sigala, Rachel Platten and KDA featuring Tinie Tempah and Katy B.
