What Is Rick Moranis From Honey, I Shrunk The Kids Doing Now, In 2023?

Rick Moranis is the stage name of Canadian actor Frederick Alan Moranis. He is best known for his enthusiastic roles in a number of Hollywood movies. He started out as a “shock jock,” but then he moved on to lead roles in movies and TV shows.

Some of his most well-known roles are in Strange Brew (1983), The Flintstones (1994), and Ghostbusters (1999). He has won many awards and praise for both his acting and his voice work in several animated movies. Moranis came back to acting about a decade later in a few smaller movies, but as of 2022, it has been over 26 years since he was last seen in a big hit.

Moranis, who is now 69 years old, told everyone that he was giving up acting for a very important reason. Still, in an exclusive interview, he said that he needs a strong reason to return to the big screen. He doesn’t think of himself as being retired.

Early Life

Moranis was born on April 18, 1953, in Toronto, Ontario, to Jewish parents. He went to elementary school with Geddy Lee, the famous lead singer of the band Rush. He got all of his A Levels at Sir Sandford Fleming Secondary School. In the 1970s, he started his exciting job as a disc jockey while he was still in high school. After failing as a radio DJ, he got his first big break in the entertainment business when he starred in the Canadian sketch comedy show Second City Television in the 1980s.

He was one of the “Mackenzie Brothers” with Thomas Dave. His funny portrayal of a typical Canadian man made him well-known not only in Canada but also in the United States. Moranis got more and more important roles in Hollywood comedy movies over time.

He took advantage of the chance, appearing in movies like “Ghostbusters” and keeping his reputation as a comic in shows like “Honey, I Shrunk the Kids,” “The Flintstones,” and “Parenting,” among others. He is also a voice actor who has brought some of his favorite Disney characters to life.

What Happened to Rick Moranis?

Moranis married makeup artist Ann Belsky in 1986, and they have two children together. Belsky died in 1991 from breast cancer. She was 35 years old. In a 2015 Hollywood interview, Moranis said that he had no plans to stop acting, but that he made time to be with his children.

He is also a hero parent because he quit acting to spend more time with his family and is now a stay-at-home dad to his two kids. But he quickly realized that taking care of children was too hard, so he started traveling to make movies as a single dad.
