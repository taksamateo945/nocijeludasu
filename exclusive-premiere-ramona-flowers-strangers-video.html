EXCLUSIVE: Premiere of The Ramona Flowers' 'Strangers' Video

The Ramona Flowers are one of the most exciting indie-electro bands on the UK scene at the moment. Critics are loving their brand new funky, upbeat, electro-dance single and they are winning new fans everyday thanks to their unique sound.

Earlier this year they wowed massive crowds of music lovers at huge festivals such as Glastonbury and Fuji Rocks, and also toured across Europe, Asia and the USA, gaining a strong, supportive fanbase in the process.

Speaking exclusively to us about their new single, The Ramona Flowers’ Steve Bird says: “Strangers is written about that special feeling you get when you first meet someone and wishing that you could relive that over and over again. Ironically, this was a rare song where we wrote it collectively as a band, but nobody was in the same room at the same time. We just all knew the vibe instantly, but it was a bit like we were strangers.”   

And now the Bristol based five-piece are getting ready to share the official music video for their acclaimed new single ‘Strangers’ with the world, and we here at Celeb Mix are able to bring you the exclusive premiere of the clip. The video features America’s Got Talent finalist KidtheWiz.
