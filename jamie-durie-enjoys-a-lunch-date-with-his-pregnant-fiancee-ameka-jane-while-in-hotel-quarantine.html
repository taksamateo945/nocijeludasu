Jamie Durie enjoys a 'lunch date' with his pregnant fiance Ameka Jane while in hotel quarantine

Jamie Durie has found a unique way to connect with his heavily pregnant fiancée Ameka Jane while stuck in a hotel quarantine in Sydney. 

The House Rules judge, 51, who is currently in self-isolation after returning from a trip to America, showed his devotion to the 34-year-old model on Tuesday by documenting their iso-friendly ‘lunch date’ on Instagram. 

Posting to Instagram Stories, Jamie uploaded a photo of himself posing on the balcony of his hotel room, while Ameka stood on the street below.
