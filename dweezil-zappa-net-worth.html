Dispute Regarding Trademark

Aforementioned, the band that led the tribute act for Frank Zappa by Dweezil Zappa debuted in 2006.

Furthermore, Zappa Plays Zappa also won Best Rock Instrumental Performance of the Frank Zappa instrumental classic, Peaches en Regalia.

However, in April 2016, Dweezil Zappa announced that he’d be changing the band’s name to Dweezil Zappa Plays Frank Zappa. The announcement came after family tensions over the use of the term arose. The Trust’s lawyer later told Dweezil that ‘Dweezil Zappa Plays Frank Zappa’ infringed on the Trust’s trademarks for ‘Zappa’ and ‘Frank Zappa.’

Interestingly, the Zappa siblings got dragged into an estate dispute for decades after Frank’s widow, Gail, chose not to leave her four surviving children equal shares. Furthermore, the settlement also states that the income generated by the performance of Frank’s music must be split four ways.
