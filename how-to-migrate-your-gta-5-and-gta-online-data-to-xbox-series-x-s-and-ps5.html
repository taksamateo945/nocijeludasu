How to Migrate Your GTA 5 and GTA Online Data to Xbox Series X/S and PS5

  • Open GTA 5 on your PS4 or Xbox One
  • Go to the pause menu and select “Upload Save Game.”
  • Sign in to your Rockstar Games Social Club account (if prompted) and choose “Save Game” followed by “Upload the Save Game.
  • Confirm that you wish to start uploding the saved data and wait for the upload to finish. You should receive a message when the process is completed.

    • This process will allow you to access your GTA 5 save data on your next-gen console, but it’s important to note that the data will only be “stored” for 90 days. While you won’t permanently lose the data after that point, you may need to reupload it if you wait too long.

    It’s also important to remember that you will need to use the same Rockstar account information across every device you’ll be interacting with as part of this process. If you’re trying to transfer a character or data associated with one of your Rockstar accounts to another Rockstar account you may have, this migration process will not work properly.

    When you’re ready, it’s time to proceed to the next step…

    GTA 5 – How to Download Your Xbox One and PS4 Save Data From Your Xbox Series X/S and PS5

    Once you’ve uploaded your GTA 5 data to your Rockstar account, the process of downloading it to your next-gen console will be pretty simple. You just need to follow these steps.

  • Open GTA 5 on your Xbox Sereis X/S or PS5.
  • Select the “Story” tab.
  • Sign into your Rockstar Games Social Club account (if you’re not already signed in).
  • Confirm which save data you would like to download.
  • Wait for a message that will confirm your transfer is complete. When finished, this process should automatically take you to the game’s story mode.

    • Please note that it’s also possible to download your GTA 5 save data while you’re in story mode. To complete that process, you just need to follow these steps:

  • While playing GTA 5‘s story mode in the PS5 or Xbox Series X/S version of the game, open the pause menu.
  • Select “Game” and “Download Save Game.”
  • Sign into your Rockstar account (if required) and select the save data you wish to access.
  • Confirm that you’d like to download the selected save data. When finished, this process will take you back to the story mode.

    • While either method should allow you to pick up about where you left off, it may be easier to complete the migration process before you load your campaign simply so that you can hop right into your saved game right away.
