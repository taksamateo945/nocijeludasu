Kourtney Kardashian Opens Up About Being Away from Kids While on Tour with Travis Barker

Kourtney Kardashian is opening up about being a tour wife.

For the past several days, the 44-year-old reality star has been on the road with husband Travis Barker while his band blink-182 is on tour.

On Sunday night (May 28), Kourtney admitted she’s “cried for the past 2 days” while being away from her kids.
